This is why willpower alone doesn't work. You can't think your way out of a cortisol flood. Your prefrontal cortex — the rational brain — actually goes offline when your stress response takes over. That's why you know you're safe in bed, yet your heart races anyway.

Neuroscience has identified a specific mechanism that can interrupt this cycle: Deep Touch Pressure (DTP).
