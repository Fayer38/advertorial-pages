Why Everything You've Tried Has Failed

If you've ever Googled "how to get rid of bloating," you've probably been told to do one of the following:

  • Drink more water (which feels counterintuitive when you're already retaining it)
  • Cut sodium dramatically (nearly impossible with modern food)
  • Take over-the-counter diuretics (which flush essential minerals along with the water)
  • Try restrictive diets (which leave you exhausted and right back where you started)

And if the bloating is severe enough? Your doctor might prescribe pharmaceutical-grade diuretics — medications that force your kidneys to expel water at an accelerated rate.

The problem? They work too aggressively.
