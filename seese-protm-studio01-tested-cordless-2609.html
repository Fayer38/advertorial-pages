The Setup Problem

Here's what nobody tells you in the online listings: most cordless pressure washers still assume you have a garden hose within reach.

The DeWalt DCPW550B and EGO HPW3200 both deliver serious pressure, but they expect an inlet hose attached to your outdoor spigot.

The Greenworks GPW1800HYB can pull from a bucket in theory, but the prime cycle was finicky in my tests, and suction dropped fast.
