The Hidden Cause Most Treatments Ignore and Why Your Pain Keeps Returning

When you experience chronic lower back pain, your body enters a protective cycle. The muscles around your spine begin to weaken because pain causes you to move less.

Muscle deactivation is your body trying to protect you. But it creates a devastating feedback loop. Weaker muscles mean less spinal support, putting more pressure on damaged discs and nerves.

Every painkiller, every cortisone injection, every heating pad you have ever used addresses the symptom but does nothing about this underlying muscle weakness that keeps the cycle going.
