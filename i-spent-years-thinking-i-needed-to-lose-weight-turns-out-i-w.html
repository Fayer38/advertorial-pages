"I would wake up and my face looked like I'd been stung by bees. My rings wouldn't fit. My stomach was so distended I looked four months pregnant. And every doctor just told me to 'drink more water' or 'cut the salt.' I wanted to scream."

What Our Investigation Found

We started where Rachel started — with a simple question: why do so many women feel bloated, puffy, and heavy despite doing "everything right"?

What we uncovered surprised even our most seasoned health editors.

  • Finding #1: Water retention affects an estimated 1 in 3 women of reproductive age, yet it is rarely identified as a standalone condition — it's almost always dismissed as "just bloating."
  • Finding #2: Most over-the-counter diuretics work by forcing your kidneys into overdrive, which can lead to electrolyte imbalances, dehydration, cramping, and a vicious rebound cycle where the body holds onto even more water afterward.
  • Finding #3: Traditional remedies — dandelion root, green tea extract, parsley — have been used for centuries across Europe and Asia to gently support the body's natural fluid balance. But until recently, no one had combined them into a single, properly dosed daily formula.
  • Finding #4: A new generation of plant-based de-bloating supplements has been quietly gaining traction, with thousands of women reporting visible reductions in puffiness — without the harsh side effects of pharmaceutical diuretics.

That last finding is what led us down the rabbit hole.
