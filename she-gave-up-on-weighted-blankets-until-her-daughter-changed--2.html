The Hidden Problem With Most Weighted Blankets Nobody Talks About

Here is what nobody tells you: most weighted blankets are filled with glass microbeads or plastic pellets inside polyester. Engineered for pressure, not breathability, they become unbearable for warm sleepers.

Manufacturers are not rushing to fix this. Synthetic fills and poly-cotton shells are cheap, keeping prices low and margins high. For women with hormonal temperature shifts, these materials guarantee misery.

Deep pressure stimulation is well-documented science. Distributed weight shifts your nervous system into rest mode, supporting serotonin production and lowering cortisol — the stress hormone keeping you awake at night.
