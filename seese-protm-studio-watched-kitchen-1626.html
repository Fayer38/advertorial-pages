The Real Problem

Most leaf blowers on the market weigh between eight and twelve pounds. That might not sound like much on paper.

But hold eight pounds at arm's length for fifteen minutes. For anyone with arthritis, a bad rotator cuff, or chronic back pain, that is not yard work — that is punishment.

Gas blowers add noise, fumes, and the hassle of pull-starting an engine that fights you. Corded electric models keep you tethered to an outlet twenty feet from the driveway.
