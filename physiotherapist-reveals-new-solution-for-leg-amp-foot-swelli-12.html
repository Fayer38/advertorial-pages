Your Knee Pain Isn't What You Think It Is

Here's what no one told you. The real reason your knee pain persists has almost nothing to do with cartilage wearing thin.

"Chronic knee pain after 40 is driven by persistent inflammation trapped in the synovial membrane. The cartilage damage on X-rays is the result — not the cause. Treating the surface while ignoring the inflammatory cycle beneath is why most interventions fail."

Think of your knee joint as a house. Most treatments — creams, braces, painkillers — are painting the outside walls while a fire burns in the basement. The synovial membrane traps inflammatory compounds called cytokines that continuously signal pain and accelerate cartilage breakdown. Until you reduce this deep inflammatory cycle at the source, no surface-level treatment will provide lasting relief.
