Ceylon Cinnamon And Fasting Glucose Support

A randomized controlled trial published in a leading nutrition journal followed adults with elevated fasting glucose readings over twelve weeks, comparing Ceylon cinnamon supplementation against a matched placebo arm. Berberine At Clinically Studied Doses

A meta-analysis pooling fourteen randomized trials examined berberine supplementation in adults with metabolic dysregulation, focusing on doses of 500mg administered with meals.
