The Real Issue

To understand why oral antifungals carry such heavy warnings, you have to understand how toenail fungus actually lives.

The infection burrows beneath the keratin plate — the hard, dense, layered armor that makes a toenail a toenail.

That keratin barrier is exactly why most drugstore creams, polishes, and home remedies never reach the actual infection underneath.
